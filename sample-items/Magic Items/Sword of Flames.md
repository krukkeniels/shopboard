# Sword of Flames

This longsword is imbued with the power of fire. When you hit with it, the target takes an extra 1d6 fire damage.

**Effect**: +1 to attack and damage rolls, +1d6 fire damage
**Attunement**: Required
**Weight**: 3 lb.
