# Alchemist's Fire

This sticky, adhesive fluid ignites when exposed to air, creating a dangerous fire.

**Effect**: 1d4 fire damage on hit, additional 1d4 at start of target's turns
**Range**: 20 feet
**Usage**: Throw as an action
